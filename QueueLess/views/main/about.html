History

The Birth of a New Division

Before 1962, Caloocan was a municipality of Rizal Province and its schools were under the Division of Rizal. After its proclamation as a chartered city, a new schools division was formed.

The Growth of the Division

In 1970, the teaching force grew and more schools were established to accommodate students across different areas.

Integration of Three Municipalities

In 1975, schools from nearby municipalities were integrated, strengthening the division and expanding educational access.
