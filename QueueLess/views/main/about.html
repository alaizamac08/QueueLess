ABOUT THE SYSTEM

Mission

To develop a user-friendly and efficient system that streamlines enrollment and queuing processes, reduces waiting time, and improves overall service management for students and staff.

Vision

To become a reliable digital enrollment solution that removes long queues and provides fast, accessible, and organized school service systems.

HISTORY

The Birth of a New Division

Before 1962, Caloocan was part of Rizal Province. After its city proclamation, a new school division was formed, creating a structured education system for the area.

The Growth of the Division

In 1970, the teaching workforce expanded and more schools were built to accommodate increasing student population.

Integration of Municipalities

In 1975, nearby municipalities were integrated into the division, improving access and strengthening the education network.
